Accessing Deep Empathy

üßò The Emotional Memory Technique

For each emotion you'll explore, use this 3-step process:

  1. Recall: Remember a specific time you felt this emotion intensely
  2. Embody: Notice where you feel it physically (chest, stomach, shoulders?)
  3. Channel: Let that physical sensation inform your storytelling

üé≠ Voice & Body Shifts

  • Anger: Faster pace, sharper consonants, forward posture
  • Sadness: Slower rhythm, softer volume, slumped shoulders
  • Hope: Rising intonation, open gestures, lifted chest
  • Fear: Tighter voice, protective posture, quick glances

üí≠ Perspective Shifts

  • What would someone feeling this emotion focus on?
  • What details would matter most to them?
  • What would they need from others?
  • What action would feel most urgent?

üé™ Practice Moment

Say "I need your help" in three different emotional states. Notice how the same words carry completely different meaning based on your emotional state.
